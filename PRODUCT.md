# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Club and pickup T20 scorers on phones at the ground, plus spectators following along on a shared link. Scorers work one-handed, often in bright sun or patchy connectivity. Viewers glance at the score between overs.

## Product Purpose

Create a match in minutes, share one URL, and keep a live T20 scorecard accurate ball by ball. Success means the scorer never fights the UI mid-over, viewers trust the numbers, and offline balls sync when signal returns.

## Brand Personality

Clear, calm, field-ready. No spectacle: the score is the hero. Trustworthy like a groundsman's notebook, not a broadcast graphics package.

## Anti-references

- SaaS landing-page clichés (hero metrics, gradient accents, glass cards)
- Generic AI tool gray-and-green scaffold with uppercase section kickers
- Broadcast-style motion graphics or decorative cricket theming
- Dense admin dashboards or desktop-first layouts

## Design Principles

1. **Score first.** Every screen prioritizes runs, wickets, and overs above chrome.
2. **Thumb-scale scoring.** Primary actions are large, high-contrast, and reachable one-handed.
3. **Earned familiarity.** Standard patterns (buttons, forms, modals) over novelty.
4. **Honest offline state.** Tell the scorer when data is local-only; never silent failure.
5. **Quiet structure.** Section labels inform; they do not decorate.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA target. Visible focus on all controls, keyboard-operable modals, sufficient contrast for outdoor glare. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` on all transitions.
